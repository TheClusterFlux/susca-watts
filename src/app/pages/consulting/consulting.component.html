General Questions Our Clients Ask Us

How long does it take to obtain Accreditation?

Once we submit your application and supporting documents it can take anything from 3 months to 2 years. The timeline depends on whether your application and supporting documents meet the requirements, and WHERE you submit your application to.

How expensive is Accreditation?

It all depends on what you have in place, the more you have in place the better. In other words, the more you do, the cheaper it is.

If you do this by yourself, it could also cost you your time (could take months/years, depending on what you have/don't have & how many mistakes you make).

Before asking for assistance you must know what qualifications you want to offer. Always keep in mind, To be an accredited provider is expensive - quality learning, high standards, and subject matter experts, assessors and moderators have never been cheap.
