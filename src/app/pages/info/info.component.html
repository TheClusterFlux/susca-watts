Our Mission

At Susca Watts Academy, we are dedicated to providing quality education and professional development services that empower individuals and organizations to achieve their full potential. We believe in making education accessible, practical, and relevant to today's challenges.

Our History

Founded in Middelburg, South Africa, Susca Watts Academy has grown from a small tutoring service to a comprehensive educational institution offering a wide range of academic courses and business services. Our growth is built on our commitment to excellence and our focus on student success.

2010

Susca Watts Academy was established

2015

2020

2025

Celebrating our continued growth and success
